Chosen Scenario and Additional Elements
Scenario Selected:
Third-Person Top-Down Game

Additional Elements Selected:
Player Power-Up Pickups (minimum 3 types)
Moving Enemies (2 types)
Stationary Enemies (2 types)
Stationary Obstacles (4 types)


Brainstorming (Game Design)
The team decided to create a stealth/action-based top-down game with a dark, muted theme.

Objective: Navigate through a facility and reach the goal while avoiding enemies
Level Design:

One level with at least 5 rooms
Indoor themed environment
Clear starting point and ending goal


Gameplay Features:

Enemy patrol and stationary hazards
Power-ups such as health or temporary boosts
Obstacles that block or affect player movement


Art Style:

Dark and muted tones
Consistent visual theme
